Established in 2017, CMD is a dynamic player in India's economic growth of sectors like Power & Energy, Automobiles, Agriculture, Railways, General Engineering, Machine Tool, Defense, ISRO, Pump, valve and Marine/Aerospace etc... With a team led by seasoned experts, our promoters possess over 15 years of invaluable experience in the design, manufacturing, assembly, testing and validation of work holding fixtures. Our expertise spans the realms of hydraulic, pneumatic, and mechanical fixtures, as well as specialized solutions like Special Purpose Machines (SPMs). Moreover, we excel in process automation & robotic integration, offering specialized equipment’s to enhance efficiency and productivity across industries. At CMD, our commitment to excellence is fueled by this rich experience, driving us to deliver cutting-edge solutions that transform industries and contribute to India's progress.
